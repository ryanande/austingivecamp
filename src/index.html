    Charity Meet and Greet

    Two week prior to the coding weekend we all get together for a meet and greet, Dev's and Charities.

It is an awesome experience

Join us this fall for the Austin GiveCamp: software professionals coming together to donate time and code to deserving charities. Whether you're a developer, designer, or database admin, sign up and join in to help local non-profits and charities in your neck of the woods!

F.A.Q

What to expect.

  • Should I bring my own computer ?
    Yes. Bring your own rig!

  • Will meals be provided ?
    Yes. Continental Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner.

  • I am vegetarian/ vegan/ gluten free/ have a food allergy, etc. What should I do?
    Please let us know if you have dietary restrictions. We can't promise that we will be able to accommodate you, but we will keep you in mind and will let you know closer to the event if we can accommodate your restrictions. We will have a fridge and microwave oven that you can use if the provided food will not work for you. kristi@headspring.com
